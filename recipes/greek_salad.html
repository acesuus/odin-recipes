Greek Salad

Home

Description

A refreshing and healthy salad made with fresh vegetables, olives, and feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Kalamata Olives
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Chop cucumbers, tomatoes, and red onion
  2. Combine all vegetables in a bowl
  3. Add olives and crumbled feta
  4. Drizzle with olive oil
  5. Sprinkle with oregano and salt, then toss and serve
